What is Hack Kansas City?

Part of the National Day of Civic Hacking, HackKC brings together urbanists, hackers, government staff, developers, designers, community organizers and regular people to collaborate in building new solutions using publicly-released data, open technology and design processes.

Unlike traditional hackathons, Hack KC starts with a two-day event and continues into the fall as we will work together to bring projects to successful completion. Even after the hackathon is over, Code for KC will support your team and help you create a great civic project, from concept to beta.

Who should come?

Everyone can participate! We seek people who care about the neighborhood and community. Ideally, we need:

  • People with vision and passion
  • People with experience in all sorts of areas (see project ideas below)
  • Usability designers and graphic designers
  • Writers
  • Coders and testers
  • You!

What can I work on?

We have a few projects planned, but need your ideas, too! We have already engaged Historic Kansas City, the City of KCMO, Community Capital Fund and others willing to support our projects. We just need coders like you to lend a hand and a day or two to bring these ideas (and others) to life!

How do I get involved?

  1. Sign up yourself or your team. It's free!
  2. Submit a project idea if you have one. If not, we will help you find a team.
  3. Check out our existing projects »

In the weeks prior to June 4, we will schedule project meetings (either in person, by phone or online). We will be sure each team has the information and equipment needed, and ready to go for Hack KC.

Individuals are welcome to show up June 4-5 for a fun weekend of coding for a better metro!

What will I get out of it?

Solve local problems with your ideas and experience! Do you have ideas to improve your town? Can you make things better if you had the chance? Bring your ideas and make a positive change in your community.

You'll expand your network. HACK KC has members from a wide variety of industries, businesses and interests; developers, writers, project managers and many other professionals. Some are learning and others are experienced - all are welcome!

You'll learn new skills. With a whole weekend dedicated to letting your creative juices flow, HACK KC is a perfect opportunity to work on something new, or a little different than you have done in the past; perhaps a new platform, learn a new programming language, or give marketing a try.
